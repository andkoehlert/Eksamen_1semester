What awaits you?

Fun time

Join and fire up the grill with us for an exciting day and a culinary experience. It’s like having fun at your backyard BBQ party!

Kids BBQ

Try the perfect pastime for families or groups of friends looking to introduce their children to this activity. At Kids’ Que they can also discover the joy of grilling.

Delightful Flavours

Soothe your senses by enjoying the smell of barbecued food wafting by, and complete the experience with tasting our range of spicy, smoky, savoury and sweet grilled meat.

True Passion

Check out the art of barbecue while uncovering the perfect bites of tender meats created by genuine dedication and major talent by our competing groups.

For competitors

Do you want to compete?

The Danish Team ranked first place will get an invitation to the Jack Daniel’s World Championship Invitational BBQ which is the World Championship in American BBQ. It’s not only a huge experience, but also a great opportunity. This competition can put your Team on the BBQ map as Danish Champion in American Barbeque and as a participant in the Jack Daniels World Championship Invitational BBQ as well.

For visitors

Got kids?

The excitement of grilling can impact children’s entire life. Learning how to barbeque in a friendly and safe environment can also help kids develop social skills and creative thinking. It’s so much fun to barbecue outdoors with happy laughter, juicy steaks, crispy burgers in the presence of family and friends. At LongHorn BBQ children also have the opportunity to try out this super activity.

About us

Who are we?

The passion for BBQ, the fun and the family-friendliness are our keywords. We strongly believe that barbecuing is more than just a form of cooking; it also is a social activity. LongHorn BBQ will take place on 13 and 14 May 2023, but as early as 12 May we can expect that there will be a stream of Teams already showing up as well as beginning to see up their areas, and otherwise just enjoying themselves together or talking to the audience about barbecue.
